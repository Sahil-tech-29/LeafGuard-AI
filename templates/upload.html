About LeafGuard AI

This tool helps farmers detect nutrient deficiencies in crop leaves. Upload a leaf image and the system will analyze it and suggest the correct fertilizer.

🌾 How To Use LeafGuard AI

📸 Take Photo

Take a clear photo of a single crop leaf in good daylight.

⬆ Upload Image

Upload the leaf image using the upload box above.

🧠 Get Recommendation

The AI analyzes the leaf and suggests the correct fertilizer.

🌿 Common Nutrient Deficiencies

Nitrogen

Leaves turn yellow and growth slows.

Fertilizer: Urea

Phosphorus

Leaves may appear purple or dark.

Fertilizer: DAP

Potassium

Leaf edges turn brown or burnt.

Fertilizer: MOP

Zinc

Yellow stripes appear between veins.

Fertilizer: Zinc Sulphate
